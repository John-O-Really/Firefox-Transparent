Black and white tinted transparency for Firefox

Oh man, it's like 8am right now. This was a weird over night obsession experiment. Stuff's gonna be broken.

I was all like, "Curves? Subtle shades of sky blue? Nah man, screw this." So I search for something like this, but they all went way overboard with the changes.

This has only been tested with Windows 7 with Firefox 34.0

Black

Stuff's more readable with this one, make your browser look all sexy and stuff.

Mmm, just wait till you show your crush, they'll be all like "Damn, are those subtle text shadows to make the text readable at all times?" and you'll be all like "You betcha baby."

It could still use a few tweaks that I may get round to. Namely the selected tab can be a bit tough to find with a dark wallpaper.

White

I made this one first, there's a reason I made the black one.

I only really made these for myself, so the white theme could use a little sprucing up. It's fine for casual use with the right background.
